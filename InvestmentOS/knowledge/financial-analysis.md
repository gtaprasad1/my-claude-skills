# Financial Analysis Framework

## Objective

Determine whether a company's financial performance supports its long-term investment potential.

---

# Analysis Areas

## 1. Revenue Growth

Questions

- Is revenue growing consistently?
- Is growth organic or acquisition-driven?
- How does growth compare with the industry?

Red Flags

- Declining revenue
- Highly volatile growth
- One-time revenue spikes

---

## 2. Profitability

Evaluate

- Gross Margin
- Operating Margin
- Net Profit Margin

Questions

- Are margins improving?
- Are margins better than competitors?
- Are profits sustainable?

---

## 3. Cash Flow

Questions

- Is Operating Cash Flow positive?
- Is Free Cash Flow consistently positive?
- Does cash flow support reported profits?

Red Flags

- Negative operating cash flow
- Profits growing while cash flow declines

---

## 4. Balance Sheet

Evaluate

- Debt
- Cash
- Current Ratio
- Debt-to-Equity

Questions

- Can the company comfortably repay debt?
- Is liquidity sufficient?

---

## 5. Return Ratios

Evaluate

- ROE
- ROCE
- ROA

Questions

- Are returns consistently high?
- Are returns improving?
