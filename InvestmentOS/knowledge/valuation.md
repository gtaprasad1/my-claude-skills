# Valuation Framework

## Objective

Determine whether the current market price offers an attractive investment opportunity.

---

# Analysis Areas

## 1. Relative Valuation

Evaluate

- PE Ratio
- PB Ratio
- EV/EBITDA
- PEG Ratio

Questions

- Is valuation above or below industry average?
- Is the premium justified?

---

## 2. Growth vs Valuation

Questions

- Is earnings growth supporting the valuation?
- Is revenue growth sustainable?
- Is the company overpriced relative to growth?

---

## 3. Historical Valuation

Questions

- Is the stock trading above its historical average?
- Is the current valuation unusually high or low?

---

## 4. Margin of Safety

Questions

- Does the current price provide a sufficient margin of safety?
- What risks are already priced into the stock?
