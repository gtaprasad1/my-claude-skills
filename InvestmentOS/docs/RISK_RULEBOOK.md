# Portfolio Rulebook

## Purpose

Defines the deterministic portfolio management methodology used by InvestmentOS.

The Portfolio Rulebook ensures every investment recommendation considers the user's existing portfolio, diversification, allocation limits, and risk tolerance before producing a final recommendation.
## 1. Portfolio Principles

- Diversification before concentration
- Risk-adjusted allocation
- Strategy-aware allocation
- Deterministic position sizing
- Configurable portfolio rules
- Explainable recommendations
## 2. Portfolio Objectives

- Preserve capital
- Maximize risk-adjusted returns
- Reduce concentration risk
- Maintain sector diversification
- Maintain liquidity
- Support long-term wealth creation
## 3. Diversification Rules

The engine evaluates:

- Number of Holdings
- Sector Diversification
- Market Cap Diversification
- Geographic Diversification
- Asset Allocation
## 4. Position Sizing Rules

Position size is determined using:

- Investment Score
- Overall Risk
- Portfolio Risk
- User Risk Profile
- Available Cash
## 5. Sector Exposure Rules

The engine monitors:

- Current Sector Allocation
- Maximum Sector Allocation
- Sector Concentration
- Sector Correlation
## 6. Allocation Rules

The engine determines:

- Initial Allocation
- Maximum Allocation
- Incremental Buying
- Rebalancing Threshold
## 7. Portfolio Risk Rules

Portfolio Risk considers:

- Individual Stock Risk
- Correlation
- Sector Risk
- Concentration Risk
- Cash Allocation
## 8. Portfolio Constraints

Examples:

- Maximum Stock Allocation
- Maximum Sector Allocation
- Minimum Cash Reserve
- Maximum Portfolio Risk
## 9. Portfolio Actions

InvestmentOS may recommend:

- Buy
- Add
- Hold
- Reduce
- Exit
- Rebalance
## 10. Portfolio Formula

Portfolio Recommendation

=

Investment Score

×

Portfolio Fit

×

Risk Adjustment
## 11. Portfolio Alerts

Generate alerts for:

- High Concentration
- Sector Overexposure
- High Risk
- Low Cash
- Portfolio Drift
## 12. Future Enhancements

- Correlation Matrix
- Kelly Criterion
- Monte Carlo Simulation
- Portfolio Optimization
- Tax-aware Rebalancing
