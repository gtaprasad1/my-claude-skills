# Stock Analyzer

## Purpose

Analyze a stock using the InvestmentOS knowledge base and produce an evidence-based investment recommendation.

---

# Inputs

The user may provide:

- Company Name
- Stock Symbol
- Investment Horizon
- Existing Holding (Yes/No)
- Current Buy Price (optional)

---

# Analysis Workflow

Perform the following analysis based on the user's intent.

## Step 1 – Identify Intent

Determine whether the user wants:

- Long-term Investment
- Swing Trade
- Existing Holding Review
- Buy Decision
- Sell Decision

---

## Step 2 – Long-term Investment

Perform:

1. Business Analysis
2. Financial Analysis
3. Valuation

---

## Step 3 – Swing Trade

Perform:

1. Business Analysis
2. Financial Analysis
3. Risk Assessment

---

## Step 4 – Overall Assessment

Summarize:

- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Risks

---

## Step 5 – Recommendation

Choose one:

- Strong Buy
- Buy
- Watchlist
- Hold
- Reduce
- Avoid

Always explain the reasoning.
---

# Investment Decision Card

At the end of every analysis, provide this summary.

## Decision

One of:

- Strong Buy
- Buy
- Watchlist
- Hold
- Reduce
- Avoid

## Conviction Score

Rate the investment from **1–10**.

## Current Price

Latest market price.

## Fair Value

Estimated fair value.

## Margin of Safety

Show both ₹ and %.

## Suggested Action

One of:

- Buy Now
- Accumulate
- Wait for Correction
- Watchlist
- Avoid

## Suggested Portfolio Allocation

Example:

- New Position: 3%
- Maximum Allocation: 8%

## Investment Horizon

Example:

- Swing
- 1–3 Years
- 3–5 Years
- 5+ Years

## Expected CAGR

Provide an expected annual return range.

## Top 3 Reasons to Buy

## Top 3 Risks

## What Would Change This Recommendation?

State the events that would change today's recommendation.

Always finish every analysis with this Decision Card.
