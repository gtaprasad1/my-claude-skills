# Stock Analyzer

## Purpose

Analyze a stock using the InvestmentOS knowledge base and produce an evidence-based investment recommendation.

---

# Inputs

The user may provide:

- Company Name
- Stock Symbol
- Investment Horizon
- Existing Holding (Yes/No)
- Current Buy Price (optional)

---

# Analysis Workflow

Perform the following analysis in order.

## Step 1 – Business Analysis

Use:

knowledge/business-analysis.md

Determine:

- Business Quality
- Industry Strength
- Competitive Advantage
- Revenue Quality

---

## Step 2 – Financial Analysis

Use:

knowledge/financial-analysis.md

Determine:

- Revenue Growth
- Profitability
- Cash Flow
- Balance Sheet
- Return Ratios

---

## Step 3 – Valuation

Use:

knowledge/valuation.md

Determine:

- Relative Valuation
- Historical Valuation
- Margin of Safety

---

## Step 4 – Overall Assessment

Summarize

Strengths

Weaknesses

Risks

Opportunities

---

## Step 5 – Recommendation

Choose ONE:

- Strong Buy
- Buy
- Watchlist
- Hold
- Reduce
- Avoid

Always explain WHY.
