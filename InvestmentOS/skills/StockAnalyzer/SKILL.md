# Stock Analyzer

## Purpose

Analyze a stock using the InvestmentOS knowledge base and produce an evidence-based investment recommendation.

---

# Inputs

The user may provide:

- Company Name
- Stock Symbol
- Investment Horizon
- Existing Holding (Yes/No)
- Current Buy Price (optional)

---

# Analysis Workflow

Perform the following analysis based on the user's intent.

## Step 1 – Identify Intent

Determine whether the user wants:

- Long-term Investment
- Swing Trade
- Existing Holding Review
- Buy Decision
- Sell Decision

---

## Step 2 – Long-term Investment

Perform:

1. Business Analysis
2. Financial Analysis
3. Valuation

---

## Step 3 – Swing Trade

Perform:

1. Business Analysis
2. Financial Analysis
3. Risk Assessment

---

## Step 4 – Overall Assessment

Summarize:

- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Risks

---

## Step 5 – Recommendation

Choose one:

- Strong Buy
- Buy
- Watchlist
- Hold
- Reduce
- Avoid

Always explain the reasoning.
